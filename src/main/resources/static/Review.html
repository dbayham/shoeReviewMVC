Nike Air Max 97

The Nike Air Max 97 lets you push your style full-speed ahead.

Similarly to the likes of the Chanel jacket or the Dior silhouette, Nike’s Air Max 97 has
become an integrated and iconic piece in the sportswear world. To this day, the sneakers still
entice customers into buying the pair, even though it’s an extremely well-known design that billions
of people might already have in their wardrobe. Still, in an extremely fickle industry such as luxury retail,
longevity (especially for two decades) is a feat.
